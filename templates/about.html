About Resurrexi Labs

Computational Research Division

Research infrastructure for adversarial systems analysis. Part of Farzulla Research.

Charter & Mandate

Resurrexi Labs operates under a specific mandate: to build the systems that break the theories.

While Farzulla Research focuses on theoretical frameworks and formal academic publication, Resurrexi Labs is the engineering engine. We operate in the gap between "in theory" and "in production"—the messy, adversarial reality where systems fail.
